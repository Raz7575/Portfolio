Projects I've worked on

Below are four recent assignments / projects I completed for my courses. Each entry has a short description of what I did and the skills I practiced.

Project 1 - Business Purchase Plan

I researched a small business for sale in Oshawa and created a plan to run it successfully. The work included estimating monthly sales, listing staffing needs and salary costs, forecasting supplies and operating expenses (rent, utilities, insurance), and completing a SWOT analysis.

Project 2 - Grocery Retailer Comparison

I compared Loblaw Companies Limited and Empire Company Limited on branding, ethics, and corporate social responsibility. I reviewed recent controversies (e.g., price issues and labeling), summarized public and legal reactions.

Project 3 – CPA vs CFA Research

I researched professional finance designations (CPA and CFA). The project explains the steps to achieve each designation in Ontario, typical job titles and salary ranges, and key IT/risk-management skills required.

Project 4 – Statistics & Probability Assignment

I performed descriptive statistics (mean, median, variance, standard deviation, Q1, Q3) for a dataset, solved probability questions from a health-coverage contingency table, and interpreted a student-hours histogram.
